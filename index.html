Frequently Asked Questions

What is this assessment for?

This assessment helps you reflect on your personal values, boundaries, and comfort levels regarding sexuality and intimate relationships. It's designed for self-awareness and personal growth, helping you understand your own needs and preferences better.

Is my information kept private?

Yes, absolutely. This assessment runs entirely in your browser. Your answers are not stored on any server or database. When you close the page, your responses are gone. We don't collect any personal information.

How long does it take to complete?

The assessment contains 10 simple questions and takes about 5-7 minutes to complete. You can take your time and there's no rush. You can also pause and return later if needed.

What if I'm uncomfortable with a question?

You can skip any question you're not comfortable answering. The assessment is designed to be a helpful tool, not a stressful experience. Your comfort and boundaries are the most important consideration.

Can I retake the assessment?

Yes, you can retake the assessment as many times as you like. Your values may change over time, so it can be helpful to revisit occasionally to see if your perspectives have evolved.
